## 🛠️ کامپوننت‌های Entity Matching

### 1. EntityDisambiguator (`services/entity_disambiguator.py`)

**Thresholds**:
- HIGH_CONFIDENCE: ≥ 0.85 (مطمئن)
- LOW_CONFIDENCE: < 0.50 (رد)
- MIN_WORD_OVERLAP: 0.40

**عملکرد**:
- محاسبه word overlap
- محاسبه combined score
- تشخیص نیاز به تایید کاربر

### 2. SemanticEntityMatcher (`services/semantic_entity_matcher.py`)

**Thresholds**:
- HIGH_CONFIDENCE: ≥ 0.85
- MEDIUM_CONFIDENCE: ≥ 0.65
- LOW_CONFIDENCE: ≥ 0.45

**عملکرد**:
- Embedding-based semantic matching
- Primary keyword matching
- Cache هوشمند

### 3. HybridQueryAnalyzer (`services/hybrid_query_analyzer.py`)

**عملکرد**:
- `fuzzy_match_entity()` با شرایط سخت‌تر
- Primary keyword detection
- Combined scoring
